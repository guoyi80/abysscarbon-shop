FAQ

Common Questions

How long does shipping take?

DHL Express delivers to the US and EU in 5–10 business days. Rest of world: 7–15 business days. Processing takes 1–3 days. Tracking number within 48 hours of shipment.

What if the fins don't fit?

30 days to return any unused item in original packaging. Full refund minus shipping. Custom footpocket sizing is non-refundable unless defective. Measure carefully.

Are these products competition legal?

Yes. C1 Pro blades and N1 noseclip are AIDA / CMAS legal. MF1 monofin meets current CWT regulations. For world-championship level events, email us for compliance verification.

Do you dropship? Who makes the gear?

Zero-inventory model. Products are handcrafted in partner workshops in Italy, Japan, and the US. Every unit is individually QC-checked before dispatch. Artisan to you, no warehouse.

Soft, Medium, or Hard flex?

Soft: recreational divers under 70kg. Medium: bestseller, balanced for most. Hard: competitive athletes over 85kg needing max power. Tell us your weight and style — we recommend.

How do I track my order?

DHL tracking link emailed at shipment. Or log into your Snipcart customer account (auto-created at checkout) for live order status.
