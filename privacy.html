Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe / PayPal through Snipcart — we never store your full credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to process and fulfill orders, communicate order updates, and respond to inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

This site uses essential cookies for shopping cart functionality (via Snipcart) and site analytics. No advertising or tracking cookies are deployed. By using this site, you consent to the use of essential cookies.

4. Data Storage & Security

Order data is stored securely through Snipcart's infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures. Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill orders and comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@abysscarbon.com. EU residents have additional rights under GDPR; California residents have rights under CCPA.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Snipcart (payment & checkout), Stripe / PayPal (payment processing), and DHL (shipping). Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data handling.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries: privacy@abysscarbon.com
